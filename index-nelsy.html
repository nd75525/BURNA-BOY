Burna Boy, born Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu on July 2, 1991, in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, is a renowned singer and songwriter in the afrobeat genre. He gained international fame with his album "African Giant" in 2019 and won a Grammy Award for "Twice as Tall" in 2021. Influenced by Fela Kuti and Bob Marley, he is known for his unique blend of afrobeat, dancehall, reggae, and hip-hop.Burna Boy is known for his contributions to the afrobeat genre and his unique musical style that blends afrobeat, dancehall, reggae, and hip-hop. He gained widespread international recognition with his album "African Giant" in 2019 and further cemented his fame by winning a Grammy Award for "Best Global Music Album" with "Twice as Tall" in 2021. His powerful voice, compelling lyrics, and dynamic performances have made him a global music icon.
